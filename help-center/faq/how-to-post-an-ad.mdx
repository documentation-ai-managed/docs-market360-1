## Overview

Posting an ad is quick and easy on Market360.

## Steps

1. Login to your Market360 account
2. Click on "Post Ad" on the homepage
3. Fill in all the required details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Price
   - Location
4. Add clear photos of your item or service
5. Click "Submit" to publish your ad

Your ad will appear in search results for other users.
