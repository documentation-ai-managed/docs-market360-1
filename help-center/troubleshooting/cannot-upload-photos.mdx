---
title: Cannot Upload Photos
description: Fix issues with uploading images to your listing.
---

## Symptoms

- Photo upload fails or gets stuck
- Error: "Failed to upload image"

## Possible Causes

- File too large (>5MB)
- Unsupported format (only JPG, PNG allowed)
- Unstable internet

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your file size and type">
    ````
    Only upload JPG or PNG files under 5MB.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Improve connection">
    Ensure stable internet or try again later.
  </Step>
</Steps>
