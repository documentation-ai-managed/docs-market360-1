title: Creating a Market360 Account
description: Simple guide to signing up and starting to use Market360.
---

## Overview

Register to access all Market360 features.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to registration page">
    Visit `https://market360.sl/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Fill in name, email, phone, and create a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your account">
    Check your email for a verification link and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login and explore">
    Use your email and password to log in and start trading!
  </Step>
</Steps>
