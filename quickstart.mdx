## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A valid email address for account verification
- Your phone number (Sierra Leone format: +232 XX XXX XXX) for SMS confirmation
- A payment method like mobile money (Orange Money or Africell Money) or bank card for transactions
- The latest web browser (Chrome, Firefox) or Market360 mobile app from Google Play/App Store

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes using email or phone.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Market360" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and go to `https://market360.com` or download the app from your store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and select **Email** or **Phone**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email/phone, create a strong password, and agree to terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Account" icon="check-circle">
    Check your email or SMS for a 6-digit code and enter it to activate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up and Verify Your Profile

A complete profile builds trust and unlocks full features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile" icon="user">
    Log in and click your avatar > **Profile Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Personal Info" icon="info">
    Upload a profile photo, enter your full name, location (e.g., Freetown), and bio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Identity" icon="shield-check">
    Upload ID (National ID or Passport) and complete phone verification via SMS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link Payment" icon="credit-card">
    Connect your mobile money or bank account under **Payments**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Verification typically takes `<5` minutes. Verified profiles get priority in searches and higher visibility for sales.
</Callout>

## Browse and Search Listings

Discover products, services, jobs, and trades tailored for Sierra Leone.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="monitor">
    
    Use the search bar at the top:

    ```
    electronics smartphone Freetown
    ```

    Filter by category (Electronics, Services, Jobs), price (`<5000 SLL`), or location.

    Browse categories via the sidebar: **Buy & Sell** > **Vehicles** or **Hire Services**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    
    Tap the **magnifying glass** icon and search:

    ```
    fresh fish Waterloo market
    ```

    Swipe through personalized recommendations or use voice search for hands-free browsing.

    Save favorites by tapping the heart icon.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Complete Your First Transaction

Choose your role: buyer or seller.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Make a Purchase" icon="shopping-bag">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Item" icon="mouse-pointer">
        Click a listing > **Buy Now** or **Add to Cart**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review & Pay" icon="shopping-cart">
        Enter quantity, confirm shipping (or pickup), and pay via mobile money.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm Order" icon="check">
        Track status in **My Orders**. Rate the seller after delivery.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="List for Sale" icon="plus-square">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create Listing" icon="plus">
        Go to **Sell** > **New Listing**, add photos, title (e.g., "Used iPhone 12"), price (`150000 SLL`), and description.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish" icon="upload-cloud">
        Set category, location, and delivery options. Hit **Publish**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Manage Orders" icon="package">
        Respond to buyer inquiries in **Messages** and mark as shipped.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
