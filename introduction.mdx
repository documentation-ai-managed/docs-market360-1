## Overview

Market360 empowers Sierra Leoneans to buy, sell, hire, and trade with ease. You connect with local buyers and sellers for everything from fresh produce and electronics to skilled services like tailoring and transport. Our mission focuses on building a vibrant, trustworthy digital economy tailored to Sierra Leone's unique market needs, supporting local businesses and individuals with secure, mobile-first tools.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://market360.com` and register with your phone number or email. Verify via SMS for instant access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or List" icon="search">
    Search for "rice in Freetown" or post your first item. Add photos, prices in Leones, and descriptions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and Trade" icon="message-circle">
    Chat with buyers/sellers, agree terms, and complete secure payments.
  </Step>
</Steps>
